Avatar

Standard Avatars

The avatar component has 5 sizes, avatar-xl (6rem),avatar-lg (5rem) , avatar-std (4rem), avatar-sm (3rem) , and avatar-xs (2rem). 

          
            <figure class="avatar avatar-xl">
              <img src="images/customer-1.jpg" alt="" />
            </figure>

            <figure class="avatar avatar-std">
              <img src="images/customer-2.jpg" alt="" />
            </figure>

Avatar with status
